<p align="left"><img src='img://Textures/Interface/Books/Illuminated_Letters/T_letter.png'>he art or war has changed little since the Merethic Era, but the weapons have. [pagebreak]
<img src='img://Textures/Interface/Books/Illuminated_Letters/B_letter.png'>lade: Any edged slashing weapon, like a sword or dagger.

<img src='img://Textures/Interface/Books/Illuminated_Letters/B_letter.png'>lunt: Any solid weighted weapon, like a club or mace.

<img src='img://Textures/Interface/Books/Illuminated_Letters/P_letter.png'>iercing: Any pointed thrusting weapon, like a spear.
[pagebreak]
After years training new recruits I have developed a basic system to classify weapons. It makes weapon cross-training much easier for the fresh recruit and the more experienced solider can confidently grab a new weapon in battle, proper training may lack with it, general knowledge will be second nature. The following details primary weapons and several improvised weapons.
